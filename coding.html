Artist Statement

This project was based on the use of incorporating algorithmic ideas in our own work. I decided to choose the 'Multiple Particle Systems' example that I found online. I picked it because it looked like a simple stencil however the outcome was even better than I expected. Firstly, because it had to be hands free, I had to change the example to make it usable without the press of a mouse or keyboard. I also changed them into a custom class. Secondly, I adjusted the acceleration that the example had and made the particles go in all directions. After that, I played with color, position, size, and more. I split them into 6 different phases meaning that around a certain framecount (around 150-200 per phase), it will automatically change phases and the particles will have a different reaction depending on the phase. I think the hardest part was getting the set up right for this project such as implementing the phases and trying to understand what some lines of the code meant. I also used the recording function provided to us in class to make the final video. And overall, after experimenting for a long time, I was happy with the results!
